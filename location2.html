San Marcos Premium Outlets

3939 S Interstate 35, San Marcos, TX 78666 (512) 396-2201 Open M-Th from 11 a.m.-8 p.m. F-Sat from 10 a.m.-9 p.m. Sun from 12 p.m.-6 p.m.

Inside The San Marcos Premium Outlets

The San Marcos Premium Outlets encompass both the premium outlets and the Tanger Factory Outlet Center. They are located right off of interstate I-35 in downtown San Marcos. There is no competition really—outlets offer the ultimate shopping experience. There are so many choices and so many pretzel stands to choose from! With 145+ stores to explore, this is Texas’ largest outlet destination.

These outdoor outlets still bring customers that inside shopping experience. It’s always nice to feel like you are exercising and enjoying the outdoors while shopping. If you are looking for a specific store, chances are you will definitely find it at these outlets. Whether you’re a college student who only has $50 to spend or you’re intending to shop till you drop, people love to visit the outlets. Some people even drive in from neighboring cities.

People who want a change of scenery or a change of store, when it comes to clothes shopping, would visit the San Marcos Premium Outlets. There is so much to see, and so much more to shop for! San Marcos locals, college students, and visitors of San Marcos all love to spend time and money on themselves every once in a while. And with an area of 1,000,000 sq ft, one day spent here may not be enough. Make sure you have enough time to journey into the many fabulous stores.
